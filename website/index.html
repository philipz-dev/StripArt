Support

Answers to the most frequently asked questions about StripArt.

Is StripArt compatible with my LED bar?

As long as your LED bar software can import animated GIFs, you are good to go!

How do I create an animation?

Set your LED resolution, import or take a photo, choose start and end positions, adjust frame rate and appearance, then export as a GIF to your photo library.

Which photos work best?

Photos with clear contrast and minimal noise work best at low LED resolutions. Try different dithering algorithms on the Appearance screen.

Why do the results look pixelated?

That comes down to your LED bar resolution. StripArt exports at the exact pixel size of your strip, so a 32×96 bar shows 32 pixels tall and 96 pixels wide. The higher the resolution, the sharper and more detailed the animation will look.

Why can’t I save anymore?

StripArt includes 5 free exports. After that, you can unlock unlimited exports with a one-time in-app purchase.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Purchases are managed by Apple; StripArt does not handle billing directly.

StripArt cannot access my photos

Go to Settings → StripArt and allow access to Photos and Camera. Saving GIFs requires write access to your photo library.
